Powering Your OnGuard Tools

Get Your AI Generation Key

Five minutes, one time. Your key unlocks AI-generated threat narratives, deployment plans, and training curricula across the OnGuard tools.

What is the AI Generation Key?

Your AI Generation Key is an API key from Anthropic — the company behind the Claude AI that writes your security plan outputs. The OnGuard tools run entirely in your browser: when you generate a plan, your browser sends the request directly to Anthropic using your own key. Nothing passes through anyone else's servers, your facility information stays between you and Anthropic, and you pay Anthropic directly for only what you use — typical document generations cost cents.

One key works across all OnGuard tools. You create it once and can reuse it any time.

1 Create your Anthropic account

Go to console.anthropic.com and click Sign up. You can register with your email address or continue with a Google account, then verify your email.

This is Anthropic's developer console. It's a separate account from the claude.ai chat app — even if you use Claude for chat, the API key comes from the console.

2 Add credits

The key itself is free — usage is pay-as-you-go from a prepaid credit balance. In the console, open Billing (under Settings), add a payment method, and purchase a small amount of credits. $5 is plenty to start and typically covers many document generations.

New accounts sometimes include a small one-time trial credit — check your balance after signing up. You can set spending limits and auto-top-up rules in Billing so there are never surprises.

3 Create the key

  1. In the console's navigation, open API Keys.
  2. Click Create Key and give it a name you'll recognize — e.g. OnGuard.
  3. Your key appears one time only. It's a long string starting with sk-ant-. Copy the whole thing immediately.
Shown once, ever. If you close the window without copying, no harm done — just delete that key and create a new one. If you lose a key later, do the same: revoke it and create a fresh one.

4 Use it in the OnGuard tools

Open any OnGuard tool and paste the full key into the AI Generation Key field (the one showing sk-ant-...). That's it — generation is unlocked. The key is used by your browser to talk to Anthropic and is never sent anywhere else.

Keep it safe

  • Treat the key like a password — anyone holding it can spend your credits.
  • Don't email it, text it, or post it anywhere.
  • Compromised or lost? Revoke it in the console and create a new one — takes under a minute.
  • Set a spending limit in Billing as a safety net.
